Reviewer Finder — SPECTER2 cosine match against 41,975 indexed authors

Paste a paper's title and abstract. We embed it with SPECTER2 (Cohan et al. 2020 / Singh et al. 2023) in your browser, then rank authors by cosine similarity to their citation- and recency-weighted SPECTER2 author centroid. Optional: paste the bibliography to use cited papers as positive signal (DOIs are matched into the atlas corpus); enter author names/ORCIDs to flag and filter coauthors (COI). Each top reviewer ships with 1–3 of their own papers most similar to your query, so you can audit the recommendation. Everything runs locally — the query never leaves your machine.
