Collaboration Style

For each author, the fraction of their coauthors who sit in the same semantic-Leiden community. High values = topical homophily (an author collaborates within their own subfield); low values = topical brokerage (the author bridges disjoint subfields). Lift is the homophily score divided by the chance baseline (the rate at which two random authors share a semantic community).
